Ingredients

Chicken

  • Chicken
  • garlic clove
  • curry Powder
  • sesame oil
  • soy sauce
  • fresh ginger
  • Lime juice

Peanut Sauce

  • Coconut Milk
  • Peanut butter
  • garlic clove
  • palm sugar
  • Red Curry Paste
  • sesame oil
  • soy sauce
  • fresh ginger
  • Sriracha
  • Lime juice

Instructions

Chicken

  1. Add all of the ingredients to a large ziploc bag. Toss to coat and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  2. When you are ready to eat preheat grill to medium high heat.
  3. Thread the chicken pieces onto the skewers. (To keep the wooden skewers from burning I soak them in water for 30 minutes to an hour before using them.)
  4. Place on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  5. Serve with peanut dipping sauce, lime wedges and extra cilantro for garnish if desired.

Peanut Sauce

      Add all of the ingredients to a large ziploc bag. Toss to coat and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
